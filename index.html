Hi, I'm Will! I'm a behavioral ecologist fascinated by the feedback between animal behavior and parasitism. Iâ€™m currently a 5th-year Ph.D. student with Vanessa Ezenwa in the Ecology and Evolutionary Biology Department at Yale. My work focuses on the role that behavior - particularly social interactions and movement ecology - play in shaping disease outcomes in wildlife, and how parasites in turn shape their host's behavior. I'm interested in better understanding how these feedbacks scale-up to shape epidemics, social dynamics, and host ecology.
